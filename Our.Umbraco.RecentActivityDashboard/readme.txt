Thank you for installing Our.Umbraco.RecentActivityDashboard! 
This package installs a collection of dashboards into your Umbraco CMS. 
Dashboards are installed into the Content, Media, Settings and Members section.
Each dashboards show the recent activity in that section for the last 24 hours
except the dashboard in the Settings section which shows the recent activity for the
last 30 days.
